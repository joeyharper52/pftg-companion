📖 Abstract

This work develops a field-based alternative to General Relativity and Quantum Field Theory by modeling all interactions as emergent pressure dynamics. Gauge fields arise as harmonic modes of a scalar field Φ(x), with solitons representing particles. Entropy gradients in Φ(x) generate effective forces that explain lensing, CMB, and galaxy rotation without invoking dark matter.
